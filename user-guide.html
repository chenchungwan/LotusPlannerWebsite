‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! We only read your Google data (never write to it without permission), and your personal journal entries are stored securely in your iCloud account, not on our servers.

Can I use it offline?

Yes, you can view and edit your journal entries offline. Google Calendar and Tasks data will sync when you're back online.

Does it work on iPhone?

Yes! While optimized for iPad, Lotus Planner works great on iPhone with a responsive design that adapts to the smaller screen.

Can I export my data?

Your Google data remains in Google (accessible anytime), and your journal entries are in your iCloud account. We're working on additional export options.
