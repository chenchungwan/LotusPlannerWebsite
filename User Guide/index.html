Setting Up Google Integration

1. Link Your Accounts

Go to Settings and tap "Link Google Account" for both Personal and Professional accounts. Follow the OAuth flow to authenticate.

2. Grant Permissions

The app will request access to your Google Calendar and Google Tasks. Grant these permissions to enable full functionality.

3. Customize Account Names

Give your accounts custom names (e.g., "Work" and "Personal") to easily distinguish between them in the interface.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

üîê Authentication Issues

Problem: Can't sign in to Google accounts
Solution: Check your internet connection and ensure you're using the correct Google account credentials. Try unlinking and re-linking the account.

üîÑ Sync Problems

Problem: Data not syncing with Google
Solution: Check your internet connection and ensure you've granted the necessary permissions. Try signing out and back in.

üìù Journal Not Saving

Problem: Journal entries not saving
Solution: Ensure you have iCloud Drive enabled and sufficient storage space. Check your iCloud settings in iOS Settings.

üì± App Crashes

Problem: App crashes or freezes
Solution: Force close the app and restart it. If the problem persists, restart your device and check for app updates.

üí° Pro Tip: If you're experiencing persistent issues, use the "Report Issue" feature in the app menu to send detailed information to the development team.
